What is Diabetes? Types of Diabetes Symptoms Prevention Treatment

What is Diabetes?

Diabetes is a chronic disease that occurs when the pancreas is no longer able to make insulin, or when the body cannot make good use of the insulin it produces.

Types of Diabetes

  • Type 1 Diabetes
  • Type 2 Diabetes
  • Gestational Diabetes

Symptoms

  • Increased thirst
  • Frequent urination
  • Extreme hunger
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Fatigue
  • Blurred vision

Prevention

While there is no known way to prevent Type 1 diabetes, you can prevent or delay Type 2 diabetes by maintaining a healthy weight, eating a balanced diet, and exercising regularly.

Treatment

Treatment for diabetes aims to keep blood glucose levels as close to normal as possible. This involves lifestyle modifications, medication, and sometimes insulin therapy.
