Respiratory Care

Treatments

    Respiratory Infection Treatements
  • Get regular and adequate amounts of sleep
  • Drink extra water
  • Use a cool mist vaporizer or saline nasal spray to relieve congestion
  • For sore throats, ice chips, sore throat spray or lozenges can help symptoms
  • Use saline gargles for sore throat 2-4 times per day
